# LinkedIn Content Calendar

## Content Pillars
1. **Educational** (50%) - How-to guides, tutorials, demos
2. **Inspirational** (25%) - Success stories, before/after, transformations
3. **Thought Leadership** (15%) - AI trends, PM evolution, predictions
4. **Community** (10%) - Polls, questions, discussions

## Weekly Cadence
- **Monday:** Educational post (carousel or article link)
- **Wednesday:** Quick tip or demo video
- **Friday:** Community engagement (poll or question)
